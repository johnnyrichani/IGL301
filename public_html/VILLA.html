Luxury Villa

To take advantage of the beautiful woodland view, we imagine the distinctive Y-shape of the house, which creates three winds, each glazed on both sides.

There's a wing for work, study and music; one for cooking and eating; and one for living and painting.

in the basement, the Y creates a similar functional clarity: one wing is for the master bedroom; one for cars; and one the guest rooms and storage.

A patio provides light (and a separate entrance) for the guest rooms. The central area where all the wings meet is the heart of the house. It forms a large

space that serves as entrance hall, dining room, bar and music room.
